# Tsunami Numerical Modelling

Tsunami Numerical Modelling algorithms are computational techniques for solving mathematical problems approximately when analytical solutions are unavailable for tsunamis.

They are widely used in scientific computing, engineering simulations, optimization, and physical modeling.
