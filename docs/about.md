## Origin & Vision  
SGCCRfC was founded in 2013 with the goal of empowering the scientific community with **open, reliable, and accessible computing resources**. We aim to bridge the gap between scientists and the rapidly evolving world of grid, cloud, and HPC infrastructures. As well, to build a globally accessible knowledge ecosystem that bridges the gap between cutting-edge computational technologies and the scientific community, enabling innovation, research, education, and practical applications across diverse fields.

## What We Do  
- Curate scientific tools from open platforms  
- Provide public-domain educational resources  
- Promote accessible cloud & grid computing  
- Support interdisciplinary research communities  

## Who It Serves  
Scientists, engineers, data analysts, educators, students, and anyone involved in computational research.
